Dance Marathon 2015

Hundreds of people are participating in the 14th annual Dance Marathon – a fundraising event where students stay on their feet for 26 hours to raise money to fight pediatric AIDS. For the second year, the UCLA Pediatric AIDS Coalition is holding Dance Marathon in Pauley Pavilion. Stay with us for live updates, including videos, dancer profiles and photos. If you want to share your own stories, tweet or instagram with the hashtag #UCLADM2015.
