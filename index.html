DANCE MARATHON 2016

Hundreds of people are participating in the 15th annual Dance Marathon – a fundraising event where students stay on their feet for 26 hours to raise money to fight pediatric AIDS. For the third year, the UCLA Pediatric AIDS Coalition is holding Dance Marathon in Pauley Pavilion. Stay with us for live updates. If you want to share your own stories, tweet or instagram with the hashtag #UCLADM2016.
